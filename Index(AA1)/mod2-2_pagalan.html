Haikyu!!

Haikyuu is the story of a young boy named Hinata Shoyo who aspires to be a proffesional volleyball player despite his small height just like his idol, the "Little Giant". In his first and last match in junior high, he is defeated by Kageyama Tobio, a genius setter who is also known as "The King of the Court". The two quickly became rivals and Hinata vowed to defeat Kageyama in high school. Hinata is accepted by a local high school, Karasuno High. Unexpectedly, he finds his rival Kageyama Tobio in the same school. It's then revealed that Karasuno has lost it's reputation after it get's defeated after the Little Giant's era. With Kageyama's genius setting and Hinata's athleticism, they quickly rise along with their team. They meet new friends who accompanied them during thier journey. Kageyama and Hinata both pushes each other to their full potential. They have a special bond, that devottheir lives to volleyball.
